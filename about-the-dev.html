About

ALOLA! I am Dev (yeah, Dev is my name >_>). I am an indie developer working on aerial ace as a side project. Aerial Ace is the first and only discord bot i have made. It started as a small project so that i can learn how discord bot worked.

I have always been a huge pokemon fan, and was spending lots and lots of hours playing poketwo :| After playing poketwo for quite a while and realising what player needed, i decided to kick start the development of this bot. This bot was (for me) a way to learn both bot development and simultanously making something for the community that i been into for such long a time.
